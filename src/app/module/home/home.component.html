What is WorkFlow?

A WorkFlow is the process of how you get stuff done. It’s a system that makes work more efficient while keeping everyone on the same page. Workflow management is all about building and tracking these processes.
WorkFlow is like a home base for all your workflows: use it as a tool to create workflows and track your team’s work.
